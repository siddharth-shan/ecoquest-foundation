Frequently Asked Questions

How can my scout troop participate in your events?

Scout troops can register for our public events or contact us to arrange a private event. We offer conservation projects that align with various merit badge requirements and provide service hour documentation.

Are your programs suitable for classroom use?

Yes! Our digital resources and educational games are NGSS-aligned and designed for middle and high school students. Educators can access free materials through our Educator Portal.

How can students get involved?

Students can participate in our programs, volunteer at events, contribute content to our blog, or join our youth leadership team. We welcome students of all ages and skill levels!

Do you accept donations of equipment or supplies?

Yes! We accept in-kind donations of conservation supplies, educational materials, and technology. Please contact us to discuss what items would be most helpful.

How can I stay updated on new events and programs?

Subscribe to our newsletter through the footer on any page, follow us on social media, or check our Events page regularly for the latest updates.

Can my organization partner with EcoQuest Foundation?

Absolutely! We're always looking for partnership opportunities with schools, nonprofits, and community organizations. Use the contact form above or visit our Get Involved page for more information.
